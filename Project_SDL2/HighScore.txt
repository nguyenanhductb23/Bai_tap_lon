Welcome to TREASURE HUNT PRO!!!

In this game, you will get inside Werner - a hero who has an ability of walking in the air.
His target is to get the precious treasures hidden among the walls;
however, these wall are not stable so you can destroy each of them by stepping strongly on it.
On your way to the treasure, the most noticeable dangers are 6 pumpkin monsters.
If you stand next to one of them, you will lose.
